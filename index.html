About the Constructor

Nancy Serrano-Wu is a Latina (Colombian) crossword constructor and immigration attorney based in Belmont, Massachusetts.

She made her New York Times debut on Wednesday, January 25, 2023 — joining the small but growing ranks of published Latina constructors. Her puzzles aim to be inclusive and reflect a multicultural world, drawing on her family's heritage with answers like PAPI and BABA.

In addition to the NYT, she has been published in the Los Angeles Times, Wall Street Journal, USA Today's Universal puzzle, the Inkubator, AVCX, Lil AVC X, Puzzmo, the Modern Crossword, and Slate, where she contributes regularly to the daily puzzle.

Featured in the documentary on representation in crosswords, Nancy began constructing puzzles as birthday gifts and has since become a leading voice for inclusive cluing.
